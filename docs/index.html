About

Boulder has a large running community. As a newcomer (like myself), it is daunting to navigate all the meetups and events.

Boulder Runners aggregates running groups, stores, etc. for convenience. Hoping to help grow the community.

Sat, Feb 3

9:00AM

Back The Blue 5k Run/Walk from Boulder Running Company

Boulder Running Company, Castle Rock Chamber and Stridemob have joined forces in the effort to bring you the Zackari Parrish 5k Run/Walk #BacktheBlue on Saturday, February 3rd at the Douglas County Events Center. The race will coincide with the Castle Rock Chamber's Douglas County Lifestyle Expo.

The Zackari Parrish 5k Run/Walk (9 AM to 12 PM) was inspired by the sacrifice of Deputy Parrish and his Douglas County brothers behind the badge by Erin Dilbeck. The Parrish Family as well as the Fallen Officers fund are beneficiaries of the 5k. Following the race, runners can enjoy a pancake breakfast prior to entering the Douglas County Lifestyle Expo (where the awards ceremony will be held) they can enjoy vendor booths and ongoing entertainment.

Bring your spectators, the expo is free and open to public and will include a kid's obstacle course, interactive displays and more. To learn more details about the 5k race and to register visit:
http://zackariparrish5k.itsyourrace.com/event...

The Douglas County Lifestyle Expo, organized by the Castle Rock Chamber of Commerce, is a one day event held at the Douglas County Events Center (8 AM to 2 PM) that inspires and educates community residents to Live, Work, Play in Douglas County. This event showcases all things great about Douglas County, highlights businesses and non profit organizations that support our community and celebrates them. The Boulder Running Company, Castle Rock Chamber and Stridemob are using this day to collaborate and come together as a community and support each other.

The Douglas County Lifestyle Expo is a free event, bring the entire family for a day of family fun. View more details about the event and vendor registration can be found at https://castlerock.org/dc-lifestyle-expo/.

For those who are unable to attend the race but would still like to donate, please visit: http://www.dcsheriff.net/fallen-officer-fund/...

Thank you for assisting us to raise money for the Parrish Family & the Fallen Officers Fund. Please Note: All ages are welcomed and all animals too but please make sure animals are on a leash and be advised that pets are not allowed inside the Douglas County Events Center (only service dogs permitted).
